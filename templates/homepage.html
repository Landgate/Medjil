Medjil, which means ‘accurate’ in the Whadjuk Noongar language, is a survey instrumentation calibration portal designed for the rigorous calibration of Electronic Distance Measurement Instrumentation (EDMI) and barcode levelling staffs. To proceed with the calibration of your instrument, please refer to the user guides available under the Resources tab.

The provided calibration laboratories, such as the EDM Baseline or the Staff Calibration Range, are managed by NMI-accredited organizations. Medjil allows authorized users to calibrate both types of laboratories.

The Technical Manual is designed to provide an in-depth understanding of the underlying theories, mathematical formulas and assumptions used by the system to perform the calibrations.
