Medjil means accurate in Whadjuk Noongar language, a term carefully adopted to provide a system to rigorously calibrate the Electronic Distance Measurement (EDM) in the Total Station and the levelling staff used in spirit levelling. To proceed with instrument calibration, please click on the Resources tab and read the respective user guides carefully.

The User Guides provide a step-by-step instructions for calibrating an instrument - be it an EDM or a levelling Staff. The calibration laboratories (e.g., EDM Baseline or the Staff Calibration Range) are calibrated as required using calibrated instruments to provide an accurate and up-to-date parameters used for calibrating your instrument.

The Technical Manual is designed to provide an indepth understanding of the underlying theories, mathemical formulas and assumptions used by the system to perform the calibrations.
